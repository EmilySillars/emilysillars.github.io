Bambara Beans Secret Message Writer

A bean-themed secret message reader and writer!
Project completed Summer 2017.

This desktop application encrypts and decrypts plain text messages into the Poulin Cipher, a method of secret writing devised by my dear friend and cipher extraoridinaire, Callum Poulin.
Press the down arrow "Boss Key" to conceal your work as an innocent bean-counting videogame!
All UI and functionality programmed by Emily.
